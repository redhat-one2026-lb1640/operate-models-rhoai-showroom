From his high tower, Aerendil watched the data-streams turn from the crimson of battle to the steady green of victory. His war was fought in silence, in a realm of pure logic, but its results were as real as the stones of the city he had helped to save. The shadow had been met with light, and the cleverness of the enemy had been answered with the wisdom of the machine, guided by the hand of a master.
